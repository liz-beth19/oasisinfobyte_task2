About Me - Lizbeth Mokgala

Hello, I'm Lizbeth Mokgala

I'm currently in my final year pursuing a Bachelor of Science in Information Technology at Richfield Graduate Institute of Technology. My primary interest lies in cybersecurity, where I aim to contribute to making digital spaces more secure and resilient against threats.

While cybersecurity is my main focus, I also have a solid understanding of web development. I enjoy creating web applications and websites, leveraging my skills to craft user-friendly experiences.

Throughout my academic journey, I've gained valuable insights into various aspects of technology, including programming languages, network security, and database management. I believe in continuous learning and staying updated with the latest trends in the ever-evolving field of IT.

Outside of academics, I'm passionate about contributing to open-source projects, participating in hackathons, and exploring innovative solutions to real-world problems.

Feel free to browse through my portfolio to see some of my projects and experiences. If you'd like to connect or collaborate, don't hesitate to reach out!
